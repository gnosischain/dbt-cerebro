int_execution_circles_v2_trust_pair_ranges

Validity ranges for Circles v2 trust relations. One row per (truster, trustee) pair, with parallel arrays valid_from_agg/valid_to_agg holding the intervals during which trust was active, plus range_count. Built by sorting each pair's events from int_execution_circles_v2_trust_updates and closing every interval at the earlier of its expiry_time and the next update's start; the final open interval ends at the DateTime max sentinel (2106-02-07), and zero-length ranges are dropped.
