api_mixpanel_ga_growth_campaign_weekly

GROWTH — campaign-attributable Gnosis App conversion funnel by UTM campaign x week. Aggregate-only projection of api_mixpanel_ga_gnosis_app_acquisition_weekly with the causal-validity treatment applied: first_touch only, clipped to week >= 2025-10-01 (Mixpanel era), and the touch-precedence gate (a campaign is credited only when its touch preceded the conversion). conversion_kind in (topup, starts_referring, swap_filled, token_offer_claim); topup is the attributable "funded" proxy. Only ~4-7% of in-era app conversions carry a causally-valid campaign; the rest are utm_campaign='unknown'. Served via the metrics-dashboard x-api-key layer (blanket-excluded from cerebro-api).
