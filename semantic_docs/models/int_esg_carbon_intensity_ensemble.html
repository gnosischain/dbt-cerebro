int_esg_carbon_intensity_ensemble

The `int_esg_carbon_intensity_ensemble` model aggregates and enhances uncertainty estimates for country-level electricity carbon intensity data, supporting ESG and sustainability analysis.

Module: ESG

Status: candidate

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/ESG/intermediate/int_esg_carbon_intensity_ensemble.sql

Tags: production esg carbon_intensity

Columns

NameTypeDescription
base_carbon_intensityFloat64Raw base estimate of carbon intensity in gCO2/kWh.
base_confidenceFloat64A fixed confidence level (0.85) indicating the reliability of the data source.
calculated_atDateTimeTimestamp when the data was calculated.
carbon_intensity_gco2_kwhFloat64The measured carbon intensity in grams of CO2 equivalent per kilowatt-hour.
carbon_intensity_meanFloat64Estimated average carbon intensity in gCO2/kWh, seasonal adjusted.
carbon_intensity_stdFloat64Standard deviation representing combined temporal and measurement uncertainty.
ci_lower_90Float64Lower bound of the 90% confidence interval.
ci_lower_95Float64Lower bound of the 95% confidence interval.
ci_upper_90Float64Upper bound of the 90% confidence interval.
ci_upper_95Float64Upper bound of the 95% confidence interval.
coefficient_of_variationFloat64Ratio of the combined standard deviation to the mean, indicating relative uncertainty.
confidence_scoreFloat64Confidence score assigned to the data (0 to 1).
continentStringThe continent to which the country belongs, used for seasonal adjustment factors.
country_codeStringThe ISO 3-letter country code or 'WORLD' for global data, identifying the country or region.
measurement_stdFloat64Standard deviation due to measurement uncertainty.
month_dateDateThe date representing the month for which the carbon intensity data is recorded.
n_sourcesUInt64Number of sources used.
seasonal_adjustmentFloat64Seasonal adjustment factor applied to the base estimate.
sourceStringThe data source identifier, here indicating Ember electricity data.
sources_usedStringArray of sources contributing to the data.
temporal_stdFloat64Standard deviation due to temporal variability.
uncertainty_categoryStringQualitative assessment of uncertainty ('Low', 'Medium', 'High').
