api_consensus_validators_performance_daily

Public API view joining daily income and proposer-reward facts at (date, validator_index). Filterable by validator_index, pubkey, withdrawal_credentials, withdrawal_address, date_from, date_to.

Module: consensus

Status: approved

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/consensus/marts/api_consensus_validators_performance_daily.sql

Tags: production consensus tier1 api:validators_performance granularity:daily

Columns

NameTypeDescription
apyFloat64Annualized percent yield computed from daily_rate.
balance_gnoFloat64End-of-day validator balance in GNO.
balance_prev_gnoFloat64Previous-day balance in GNO.
consensus_income_amount_gnoFloat64Spec-bounded consensus income on the date, in GNO — balance delta minus effective deposit credits, plus withdrawals, net of consolidations (see int_consensus_validators_income_daily).
consolidation_inflow_gnoFloat64Consolidation amount received (target role) on the date, in GNO.
consolidation_outflow_gnoFloat64Consolidation amount transferred out (source role) on the date, in GNO.
cumulative_consolidation_inflow_gnoFloat64Running sum of consolidation inflows for the validator, in GNO.
cumulative_consolidation_outflow_gnoFloat64Running sum of consolidation outflows for the validator, in GNO.
cumulative_deposits_gnoFloat64Running sum of effective deposit credits for the validator, in GNO.
cumulative_withdrawals_gnoFloat64Running sum of withdrawal amounts for the validator, in GNO.
daily_rateNullable(Float64)consensus_income_amount_gno / NULLIF(balance_prev_gno + effective_deposits_credited_gno + consolidation_inflow_gno, 0).
dateDateTime64Day of the income fact (start-of-day UTC timestamp). Grain is one row per (date, validator_index).
deposits_amount_gnoFloat64Raw reported deposit amount for the date, in GNO. Post-Pectra this is a request amount that the beacon chain may credit gradually via the pending-deposits queue (see int_consensus_validators_income_daily).
deposits_countUInt64Number of deposit events recorded for the validator on the date.
effective_balance_gnoFloat64End-of-day effective balance in GNO.
proposed_blocks_countUInt64Blocks proposed by the validator on the date (0 if none).
proposer_reward_attestations_gnoFloat64Proposer-reward component from included attestations, in GNO (source Gwei / 1e9).
proposer_reward_attester_slashings_gnoFloat64Proposer-reward component from included attester slashings, in GNO (source Gwei / 1e9).
proposer_reward_proposer_slashings_gnoFloat64Proposer-reward component from included proposer slashings, in GNO (source Gwei / 1e9).
proposer_reward_sync_aggregate_gnoFloat64Proposer-reward component from sync-aggregate inclusion, in GNO (source Gwei / 1e9).
proposer_reward_total_gnoFloat64Total proposer reward earned on the date, in GNO (0 if none).
pubkeyStringLowercased validator public key.
statusStringValidator lifecycle status at end of day (from int_consensus_validators_snapshots_daily).
total_income_estimated_gnoFloat64Lifetime estimated consensus income in GNO — balance + cumulative withdrawals − effective cumulative deposits − cumulative consolidation inflow + cumulative consolidation outflow.
validator_indexUInt64Beacon-chain validator index.
withdrawal_addressNullable(String)20-byte withdrawal address derived from credentials when prefix is 0x01 or 0x02; NULL otherwise.
withdrawal_credentialsStringLowercased withdrawal credentials.
withdrawals_amount_gnoFloat64Total amount withdrawn from the validator on the date, in GNO.
withdrawals_countUInt64Number of withdrawal events for the validator on the date.
