stg_crawlers_data__dune_bridge_flows

This view aggregates and standardizes data on cross-chain bridge flows from Dune, enabling analysis of token movements and net transfers across different blockchain networks.

Module: crawlers_data

Status: docs_only

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/crawlers_data/staging/stg_crawlers_data__dune_bridge_flows.sql

Tags: production staging dune bridges

Columns

NameTypeDescription
amount_tokenFloat64The amount of tokens transferred, expressed as a float.
amount_usdFloat64The USD value of the transferred tokens, expressed as a float.
bridgeStringIdentifier for the specific bridge used for the token transfer.
dest_chainStringName of the destination blockchain network.
directionStringIndicates transfer direction: 'in' for inbound to Gnosis, 'out' for outbound from Gnosis, 'xchain' for cross-chain transfers not involving Gnosis.
net_usdFloat64The net USD value of the transfer, accounting for inflows and outflows, expressed as a float.
source_chainStringName of the originating blockchain network.
timestampDateTime64The timestamp indicating when the bridge flow data was recorded.
tokenStringThe symbol or identifier of the token being transferred.
