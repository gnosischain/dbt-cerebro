fct_consensus_graffiti_cloud

The fct_consensus_graffiti_cloud model aggregates graffiti-related consensus data over various timeframes to support trend analysis and reporting within the graffiti domain.

Module: consensus

Status: candidate

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/consensus/marts/fct_consensus_graffiti_cloud.sql

Tags: production consensus graffiti

Columns

NameTypeDescription
graffitiStringIdentifier for the graffiti type or label, excluding entries labeled 'None'.
labelStringTime window label: '7D', '30D', '90D', 'All'.
v_30dDateTime64Sum of occurrences for each graffiti in the past 30 days.
v_7dDateTime64Sum of occurrences for each graffiti in the past 7 days.
v_90dDateTime64Sum of occurrences for each graffiti in the past 90 days.
v_allUInt64Total sum of occurrences for each graffiti across all available data.

Dimensions

  • graffiti (categorical)
  • v_7d (time)
  • v_30d (time)
  • v_90d (time)
  • label (categorical)

Measures

  • v_all_value (sum)
