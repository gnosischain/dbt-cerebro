fct_execution_tokens_ubo_coverage_latest

Per-token diagnostic of how much of the supply has been resolved to a real end-holder (UBO) versus still sitting in a labeled-but-undecomposed container. Materialized as a table — the underlying composition (balances_daily LEFT ANTI JOIN containers UNION ALL supply_claims, then LEFT JOIN labels over millions of rows) is too heavy for a view. After Phase 1 only Aave V3 + SparkLend reserves are decomposed; DEX vaults / Curve pools / Uni V3 NPM remain in pct_known_container until later phases.

Columns

NameTypeDescription
pct_direct_terminalFloat64Share of supply held by direct holders that are label-confirmed terminals (EOAs, Safes, bridges, gpay).
pct_known_containerFloat64Share of supply still held by labeled containers we have NOT yet decomposed. Phase 2+ work.
pct_unclassifiedFloat64Share of supply held by addresses with no label.
pct_unwound_otherFloat64Share of supply unwound but landing on an unclassified or still-container address (e.g. an aave depositor that is itself another protocol).
pct_unwound_terminalFloat64Share of supply unwound out of a container (Aave/Spark in Phase 1) into label-confirmed terminal addresses.
pct_unwound_totalFloat64Total share of supply that flowed through any unwind path (sum of pct_unwound_terminal + pct_unwound_other).
symbolStringToken symbol.
token_addressStringContract address of the token (lowercase).
token_classStringToken classification.
total_usdFloat64Total USD value of all positive-balance holders for this token on the latest snapshot date.
