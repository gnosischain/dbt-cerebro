int_execution_circles_v2_inviter_canonical

Canonical inviter per Circles v2 Human avatar, resolving the invitation-at-scale "farm". Hub.RegisterHuman records inviter = the farm's proxyInviter (infrastructure), not the real referrer; the GA InvitationModule emits RegisterHuman with (human, proxyInviter, originInviter). We remap by the invitee (human = avatar) — exact, because proxy->origin is many-to-one and 2,537 direct invites by addresses that also proxy elsewhere must not be remapped. One row per avatar. Consumed by int_execution_circles_v2_avatars (invited_by) and int_execution_gnosis_app_user_events (rule_invite_human).
