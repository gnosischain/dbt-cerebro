fct_execution_circles_v2_crc20_prices_daily

Daily aggregated executed prices for CRC20 wrapper tokens, one row per (date, crc20_token, backing_token, pool_address). Aggregates trade-level prices from int_execution_circles_v2_crc20_prices_raw and joins with int_execution_token_prices_daily to convert backing-token prices to USD.

Module: execution

Status: candidate

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/execution/Circles/marts/fct_execution_circles_v2_crc20_prices_daily.sql

Tags: production execution circles_v2 prices

Columns

NameTypeDescription
avatarStringCircles avatar address that owns this CRC20 wrapper.
backing_tokenStringAddress of the counterpart token (e.g. WXDAI, WETH).
backing_token_symbolStringSymbol of the backing token.
circles_typeUInt8Wrapper type (0 = demurraged, 1 = inflationary).
crc20_symbolNullable(String)Display symbol for the CRC20 token.
crc20_tokenStringAddress of the CRC20 wrapper token.
crc_bought_volumeFloat64Total CRC20 tokens bought (CRC was the token_bought leg) on this day.
crc_sold_volumeFloat64Total CRC20 tokens sold (CRC was the token_sold leg) on this day.
crc_volumeFloat64Total CRC20 tokens traded (sum of crc_amount across all swaps on this day).
dateDateCalendar date of the aggregated trades.
pool_addressStringPool contract address.
price_avg_in_backingFloat64Volume-unweighted average executed price in backing token units across all trades on this day.
price_avg_usdNullable(Float64)Average executed price in USD (avg of amount_usd / crc_amount per swap). No extra price join needed — int_execution_pools_dex_trades already resolves the backing token price.
price_median_in_backingFloat64Median executed price in backing token units.
price_median_usdNullable(Float64)Median executed price in USD (median of amount_usd / crc_amount per swap).
price_p10_in_backingFloat6410th-percentile executed price in backing token units (low-end filter signal).
price_p90_in_backingFloat6490th-percentile executed price in backing token units (high-end filter signal).
protocolStringDEX protocol (Uniswap V3 or Swapr V3).
trade_countUInt64Number of swap transactions involving this CRC20 token in this pool on this day.
