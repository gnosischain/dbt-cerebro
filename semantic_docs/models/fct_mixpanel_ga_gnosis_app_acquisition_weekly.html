fct_mixpanel_ga_gnosis_app_acquisition_weekly

Weekly progression of first Gnosis App conversions, broken down by Mixpanel UTM campaign. Tidy/long shape: one row per (week, conversion_kind, attribution_model, utm_campaign). conversion_kind is one of topup / swap_filled / token_offer_claim / marketplace_buy; attribution_model is 'first_touch' or 'last_touch'. Attribution logic: an account's UTM comes from joining its on-chain pseudonym to Mixpanel (user_pseudonym == user_id_hash) and reading the per-user first/last-touch campaign from int_mixpanel_ga_user_acquisition. First-touch = campaign on the account's earliest utm-tagged event, last-touch = latest, derived by ordering each user's events on event_time. Note the two-layer coverage: 70-83% of App converters exist in Mixpanel, but UTM rides only on entry/landing hits (~3% of events), so only ~2-4% of converters carry an actual campaign; the rest fall to 'unknown'. Aggregate-only (campaign-level counts, no user identifiers). Not exposed via cerebro-api (inherited exclusion); accessible to the MCP / semantic layer via the api_ view.

NameTypeDescription
attribution_modelStringUTM attribution window. 'first_touch' credits the campaign on the account's EARLIEST utm-tagged Mixpanel event; 'last_touch' credits the LATEST. UTM tags ride only on entry/landing hits (~3% of events), so each account's scattered tagged hits are collapsed to one first/last campaign by ordering on event_time (argMin/argMax over non-null utm_campaign, in int_mixpanel_ga_user_acquisition). The table stacks BOTH windows long — ALWAYS filter attribution_model to a single value before summing new_accounts, or counts double across the two windows.
conversion_kindStringFirst-conversion type: topup, swap_filled, token_offer_claim, or marketplace_buy.
cumulative_accountsUInt64Running total of new_accounts over weeks within (conversion_kind, attribution_model, utm_campaign).
new_accountsUInt64Count of accounts with their first conversion of this kind in this week.
utm_campaignStringMarketing campaign, 'unknown' for accounts with no Mixpanel UTM match.
weekDateISO week start (Monday, UTC) of the account's first conversion.
