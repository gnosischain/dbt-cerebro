fct_execution_gnosis_app_gpay_topups_cohort_monthly

First-top-up cohort × subsequent-top-up retention. Top-up definition is owned by int_execution_gnosis_app_gpay_topups (a GP "Crypto Deposit" into a currently-GA-owned wallet; see that model's docs). Modeling assumptions: * cohort_month = month of the user's FIRST top-up (so the cohort is the first-top-up cohort, NOT the onboard cohort). * initial_users (denominator) = the month-0 cohort size. Here max(users) over activity months equals the month-0 count by construction (every member tops up in their first-top-up month and later months are subsets), so the max()-anchor is correct for this model — unlike the onboard-cohort retention marts, which require the explicit onboard-cohort denominator. * retention_pct = % of the first-top-up cohort still topping up in month N.
