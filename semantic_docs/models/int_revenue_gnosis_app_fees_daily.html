int_revenue_gnosis_app_fees_daily

Daily per-user Gnosis App (Metri) fees denominated in USD. Users pay a CRC ERC-1155 fee to the Metri fee receiver address when transacting via Gnosis App. Each fee transfer is priced using the per-token CRC price from fct_execution_circles_v2_crc20_prices_daily (matched on avatar address). Tokens without a DEX price fall back to the daily median CRC price across all tokens that day. Only includes transfers from 2025-11-12 onward (Gnosis App launch). Grain: one row per (date, user) after aggregating across transfers.
