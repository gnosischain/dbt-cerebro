fct_mixpanel_ga_gnosis_app_daily

Daily-cumulative view of the Gnosis App sector. cumulative_users counts distinct addresses first-seen on or before each date. cumulative_mp_matched counts the subset of those that are also visible in Mixpanel. The coverage gap is (cumulative_users - cumulative_mp_matched) / cumulative_users. Per-heuristic daily address counts give visibility into which flow is driving sector growth on any given day. Implementation note: explicit AS aliases are required on the d.* columns in the final SELECT. Both daily_new and daily_kinds_pivot expose a `date` column, so ClickHouse keeps the qualified name `d.date` in the output projection and the config's order_by=(date) would otherwise fail to resolve. Same class of aliasing quirk as fct_mixpanel_ga_gpay_crossdomain_daily.
