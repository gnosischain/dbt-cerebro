stg_consensus__withdrawals

The stg_consensus__withdrawals model consolidates withdrawal event data from the consensus layer, enabling analysis of validator withdrawals over blockchain slots.

Module: consensus

Status: docs_only

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/consensus/staging/stg_consensus__withdrawals.sql

Tags: production consensus withdrawals

Columns

NameTypeDescription
addressStringThe blockchain address of the validator or entity initiating the withdrawal.
amountFloat64The amount of tokens withdrawn, typically in wei or the smallest denomination.
block_hashStringThe cryptographic hash of the block, serving as a unique block identifier.
block_numberUInt64The unique identifier of the block containing the withdrawal event.
slotUInt64The blockchain slot number indicating the specific point in the chain when the withdrawal was recorded.
slot_timestampDateTime64The timestamp corresponding to the slot when the withdrawal was recorded.
validator_indexUInt64The index identifying the validator associated with the withdrawal.
withdrawal_indexUInt64The sequential index of the withdrawal within the block, starting from zero.
