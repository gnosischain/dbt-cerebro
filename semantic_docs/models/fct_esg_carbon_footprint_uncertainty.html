fct_esg_carbon_footprint_uncertainty

This model calculates the uncertainty in the carbon footprint of a network by aggregating power consumption, energy, and emission data across nodes and geographic regions, providing a comprehensive view of ESG-related carbon emissions and their variability.

Module: ESG
