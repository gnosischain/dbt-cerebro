int_consensus_entry_queue_daily

This model provides daily aggregated insights into validator activation times within the consensus protocol, enabling monitoring of validator onboarding efficiency over time.

Module: consensus

Status: candidate

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/consensus/intermediate/int_consensus_entry_queue_daily.sql

Tags: production consensus entry_queue

Columns

NameTypeDescription
activation_daysUInt64Number of days between eligibility and activation, calculated as (epoch_activation - epoch_eligibility) * 16 * 5 / (60 * 60 * 24).
dateDateThe specific day for which the activation metrics are calculated, formatted as a date.
epoch_activationUInt64The epoch when the validator was actually activated.
epoch_eligibilityUInt64The epoch when the validator became eligible for activation.
meanFloat64Average activation_days for validators on the date.
q05Float645th percentile of activation_days for validators on the date.
q10Float6410th percentile of activation_days for validators on the date.
q25Float6425th percentile of activation_days for validators on the date.
q50Float64Median activation_days for validators on the date.
q75Float6475th percentile of activation_days for validators on the date.
q90Float6490th percentile of activation_days for validators on the date.
q95Float6495th percentile of activation_days for validators on the date.
validator_countUInt64Total number of validators active on the date.
validator_indexUInt64Unique identifier for each validator in the network.

Dimensions

  • validator_index (categorical)
  • date (time)

Measures

  • q50_value (sum)
  • q75_value (sum)
  • q90_value (sum)
  • q95_value (sum)
  • mean_value (average)
