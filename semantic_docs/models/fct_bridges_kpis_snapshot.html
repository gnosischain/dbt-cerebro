fct_bridges_kpis_snapshot

The fct_bridges_kpis_snapshot model provides a consolidated snapshot of bridge transaction metrics, including volume, net USD, transaction counts, and related rate changes over various periods, facilitating trend analysis and performance monitoring.

Module: bridges

Status: candidate

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/bridges/marts/fct_bridges_kpis_snapshot.sql

Tags: production bridges fct

Columns

NameTypeDescription
as_of_dateDateTimeThe date for which the KPIs are calculated, representing the most recent date in the data.
chg_net_7dFloat64Change in net value over the last 7 days compared to the previous 7 days, expressed as a percentage.
chg_rate_7dFloat64Change in rate over the last 7 days compared to the previous 7 days, expressed as a percentage.
chg_vol_7dFloat64Change in volume over the last 7 days compared to the previous 7 days, expressed as a percentage.
cum_net_usdFloat64Cumulative net value in USD up to the as_of_date.
cum_txsUInt64Cumulative number of transactions up to the as_of_date.
cum_vol_usdFloat64Cumulative volume in USD up to the as_of_date.
dDateThe date representing the snapshot point for the KPIs, formatted as a date.
distinct_bridgesUInt64The number of distinct bridges involved in transactions up to the as_of_date.
distinct_chainsUInt64The number of distinct chains involved in transactions up to the as_of_date.
net_7dFloat64Net value in USD over the last 7 days ending on the as_of_date.
net_prev_7dFloat64Net value in USD over the 7 days preceding the last 7 days ending on the as_of_date.
rate_7dFloat64Rate calculated as net_7d divided by vol_7d, representing the net-to-volume ratio over the last 7 days.
rate_prev_7dFloat64Rate calculated as net_prev_7d divided by vol_prev_7d, representing the net-to-volume ratio over the previous 7 days.
txs_7dUInt64Number of transactions over the last 7 days ending on the as_of_date.
txs_prev_7dUInt64Number of transactions over the 7 days preceding the last 7 days ending on the as_of_date.
vol_7dFloat64Volume in USD over the last 7 days ending on the as_of_date.
vol_prev_7dFloat64Volume in USD over the 7 days preceding the last 7 days ending on the as_of_date.
