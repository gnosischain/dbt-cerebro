fct_mixpanel_ga_card_funnel_weekly

Weekly card-order funnel over the signup cohort (first 'Create my profile' event that ISO week — the Growth dashboard's "Signups"). card_eligible = cohort users whose Mixpanel profile country is in seeds/card_eligible_countries.csv (EU-27 + NO/CH/GB); users with no profile or blank country count as NOT eligible. Numerators are eligible-scoped conversions landing in the SAME week: card_order_started ('Order the card' CTA) and card_ordered (/gnosis-pay/kyc page proxy). Rates use the eligible denominator, matching the Growth dashboard. Clipped to weeks >= 2026-03-02; current week excluded. Aggregate-only.
