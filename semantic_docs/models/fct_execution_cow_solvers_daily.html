NameTypeDescription
dateDateCalendar day of the per-solver aggregated metrics.

num_batchesNullable(UInt64)Number of settlement batches this solver executed on this day.
num_cow_batchesNullable(UInt64)Pure Coincidence-of-Wants batches (no AMM interactions) this solver executed on this day.
num_tradesUInt64Number of trade fills this solver settled on this day.
solverStringSolver address.
solver_is_activeNullable(Bool)Whether this solver is currently active in the AllowList.
total_tx_cost_nativeNullable(Float64)Total gas cost in xDAI across this solver's batches on this day.
unique_tradersUInt64Distinct taker addresses this solver traded for on this day.
volume_usdNullable(Float64)Total USD trade volume this solver settled on this day.
