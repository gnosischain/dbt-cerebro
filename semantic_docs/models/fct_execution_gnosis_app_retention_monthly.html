fct_execution_gnosis_app_retention_monthly

Cohort retention grid for the Gnosis App onboard cohort. Modeling assumptions: * cohort_month = month of the user's 'onboard' row (first-ever heuristic hit), NOT their first transactional activity. * activity_month = month of any subsequent non-onboard activity. Only the lower triangle is kept (activity_month >= cohort_month) so on-chain history predating the GA onboard does not create spurious cells. * initial_users (the retention denominator) = the onboard-cohort size, i.e. distinct users whose onboard month is cohort_month. It is deliberately NOT max(users) over activity months (peak-normalisation, which let retention exceed 100%) and NOT the month-0 actives: the numerator counts ANY cohort user active in activity_month (not a fixed followed sub-cohort), so only the full onboard cohort bounds it <= 100%. * retention_pct therefore reads as "% of the onboard cohort active in month N" (return/adoption), not the conditional stickiness of a followed sub-cohort. * amount_retention_pct anchors on the cohort's earliest activity-month amount (argMin over activity_month).
