fct_consensus_validators_explorer_daily

Per-operator (withdrawal_credentials) daily roll-up feeding the five daily charts on the Validator Explorer tab. Materialized as a physical table sorted on withdrawal_credentials so the API's `WHERE withdrawal_credentials = 'x'` filter prunes at read time via the primary index. Rebuilt incrementally month-by-month to stay under the cluster's 10.8 GiB memory cap during backfill. APY is balance-weighted (drops apy=0 idle validators): apy = SUMIf(apy * balance_prev_gno, 0 < apy < 200 AND balance_prev_gno > 0) / SUMIf(balance_prev_gno, 0 < apy < 200 AND balance_prev_gno > 0) falling back to 0 when no validators qualify. See api_consensus_validators_apy_mean_daily for the rationale. v3: drops post-exit rows. Without this, an exited validator keeps emitting zero-income / zero-balance rows from int_consensus_validators_income_daily forever, which the UI renders as a flat tail on the credential's balance line. Joining stg_consensus__time_helpers + status_latest and filtering `date <= genesis + exit_epoch * slots_per_epoch * seconds_per_slot` (or exit_epoch is FAR_FUTURE_EPOCH = 2^64-1, meaning "not exited") makes every chart downstream stop at the validator's actual exit.

Columns

NameTypeDescription
apyFloat64Mean APY across validators sharing this credential on the date, outliers filtered.
balance_gnoFloat64Sum of end-of-day validator balances across validators sharing the credential, in GNO.
consensus_income_amount_gnoFloat64Sum of daily spec-bounded consensus income across validators sharing the credential, in GNO.
consolidation_inflow_gnoFloat64Sum of consolidation amounts received (target role) on the date, in GNO.
consolidation_outflow_gnoFloat64Sum of consolidation amounts transferred out (source role) on the date, in GNO.
dateDateTime64Day of the roll-up (start-of-day UTC timestamp). Grain is one row per (date, withdrawal_credentials).
deposits_amount_gnoFloat64Sum of raw reported deposit amounts on the date, in GNO. Post-Pectra these are request amounts that may be credited gradually via the pending-deposits queue (see int_consensus_validators_income_daily).
effective_balance_gnoFloat64Sum of end-of-day effective balances across validators sharing the credential, in GNO.
proposed_blocks_countUInt64Total blocks proposed on the date by validators sharing the credential.
proposer_reward_total_gnoFloat64Sum of proposer rewards earned on the date by validators sharing the credential, in GNO.
validator_count_activeUInt64Count of distinct validator_index rows included in this (date, credential) aggregation. v2 (2026-04). Dashboard uses this to decide between quantile bands (N>1) and a clean rolling-median line (N=1 → solo credential) on the APY chart.
withdrawal_credentialsStringLowercased withdrawal credentials — the per-operator grouping key.
withdrawals_amount_gnoFloat64Sum of amounts withdrawn on the date across validators sharing the credential, in GNO.
