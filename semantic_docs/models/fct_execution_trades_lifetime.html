fct_execution_trades_lifetime

One-row lifetime summary of DEX trading on Gnosis Chain. Materialized as a table, full rebuild on every dbt run. Feeds the lifetime tiles on the Trades → Stats tab.

Module: execution

Status: approved

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/execution/pools/marts/fct_execution_trades_lifetime.sql

Tags: production execution pools trades fct

Columns

NameTypeDescription
lifetime_trade_countUInt64All-time distinct transaction count.
lifetime_unique_tradersUInt64All-time distinct `tx_from` count.
lifetime_volume_usdFloat64All-time DEX volume summed at swap (hop/leg) grain — each pool leg of a multi-hop route contributes its amount_usd independently, matching the volume timeseries convention (api_execution_trades_stats_volume_ts). This is a deliberately different grain from lifetime_trade_count (per-tx), so the two are not directly divisible.
