int_execution_safes

Gnosis Safe deployments on Gnosis Chain. A Safe is deployed by the SafeProxyFactory as a minimal proxy that delegatecalls into a singleton/mastercopy. The constructor immediately delegatecalls setup(...) on the singleton — that's how we identify a Safe trace: a delegatecall to a known singleton whose calldata starts with one of the historical setup() selectors. The singleton-to-version map lives in seeds/safe_singletons.csv. Covers every Safe version (0.1.0 through 1.4.1, including the Circles 1.1.1 fork and the L2 variants), including pre-v1.1.0 Safes that never emitted SafeSetup. Source: execution.traces. We use dedup_source(..., pre_filter=...) so the time-range and selector filters land BEFORE the ROW_NUMBER window function and ClickHouse can prune monthly partitions. This is the largest scan in the family (8B traces, 5+ years) — every monthly batch must fit under the CH Cloud memory budget. Address normalization: execution.traces stores addresses + calldata as lowercase hex WITHOUT a '0x' prefix. The seed values in safe_singletons are stripped of the prefix when matching, and outputs (safe_address, creation_singleton, tx_hash) are re-prefixed with '0x' so downstream models see the canonical form.
