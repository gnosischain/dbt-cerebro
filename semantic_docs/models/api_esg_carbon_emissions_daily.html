api_esg_carbon_emissions_daily

The `api_esg_carbon_emissions_daily` model provides daily aggregated estimates of carbon emissions, including moving averages and uncertainty bounds, to support ESG reporting and analysis.

Module: ESG

Status: candidate

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/ESG/marts/api_esg_carbon_emissions_daily.sql

Tags: production esg tier1 api:carbon_emissions granularity:daily

Columns

NameTypeDescription
dateDateThe specific calendar date for the recorded carbon emissions data.
ma7_lower_90Float647-day moving average of the lower 90% confidence bound for daily CO2 emissions in kilograms, indicating the lower estimate of uncertainty.
ma7_lower_95Float647-day moving average of the lower 95% confidence bound for daily CO2 emissions in kilograms, representing a more conservative lower estimate.
ma7_upper_90Float647-day moving average of the upper 90% confidence bound for daily CO2 emissions in kilograms, indicating the upper estimate of uncertainty.
ma7_upper_95Float647-day moving average of the upper 95% confidence bound for daily CO2 emissions in kilograms, representing a more conservative upper estimate.
ma7_valueFloat647-day moving average of the daily mean CO2 emissions in kilograms, providing a smoothed trend over the past week.
