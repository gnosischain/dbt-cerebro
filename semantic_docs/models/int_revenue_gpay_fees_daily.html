int_revenue_gpay_fees_daily

Daily per-user Gnosis Pay fees. Each ERC20 transfer to the GP settlement address is treated as a payment; the per-payment fee is `amount_token * fee_bps / 10000` and is then priced to USD. Per-symbol fee bps are declared as Jinja constants at the top of the SQL. Transfers are already aggregated daily per (from, to, token) in int_execution_transfers_whitelisted_daily, so we aggregate once more to the (date, from, symbol) grain and apply the per-symbol fee rate.
