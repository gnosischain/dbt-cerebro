stg_crawlers_data__dune_bridge_flows_v2

V2 staging view over the crawlers_data.dune_bridge_flows source, expecting Dune to deliver pre-aggregated daily rows (with date and txs columns). One row per source row at (date, bridge, source_chain, dest_chain, token) grain; casts amounts to Float64 (renaming amount_token/amount_usd to volume_token/volume_usd), casts txs to UInt64, and derives direction as 'in'/'out'/'xchain' from whether Gnosis is the destination or source chain.
