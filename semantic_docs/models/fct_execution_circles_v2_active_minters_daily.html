fct_execution_circles_v2_active_minters_daily

Per-day count of Circles v2 "Active Minters" — avatars that minted on each of the last 14 days AND whose 14-day mint sum is >= 80% of the 336 CRC theoretical maximum. Canonical KPI from the Dune circles-v2-kpis dashboard. Blacklisted avatars are NOT excluded here; the cohort distribution mart does the exclusion.
