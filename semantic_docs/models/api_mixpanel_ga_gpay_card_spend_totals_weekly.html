api_mixpanel_ga_gpay_card_spend_totals_weekly

GROWTH — Gnosis Pay card-spend as weekly TOTALS (no campaign dimension). Summed over campaign from api_mixpanel_ga_gpay_campaign_metrics_weekly; each account has one first-touch campaign so the partitions are disjoint and counts/USD sum cleanly. avg_payments_per_account is recomputed (a ratio, never summed). Aggregate-only; served via x-api-key.
