int_ubo_claims_curve_daily

Per-protocol UBO supply claims for Curve 3pool (0x7f90122bf0700f9e7e1f688fe926940e8839f353) on Gnosis Chain. Unwinds both direct x3CRV LP holders and depositors in the x3CRV gauge (0xb721cc32160ab0da2614cc6ab16ed822aeebc101) into a single set of final wallet-level claims. Gauge depositors receive an effective x3CRV allocation proportional to their share of total gauge supply, preserving total conservation (sum of claims = pool reserves). Sourced from contracts_Curve3PoolLP_events and contracts_CurveGauge_events Transfer events; pool reserves from int_execution_tokens_balances_daily. Backfilled from 2021-09-01 (pool deployment) in 6-month batches.
