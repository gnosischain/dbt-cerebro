fct_execution_cow_daily

Daily CoW Protocol metrics: volume, trades, unique traders, fees, batch counts, CoW ratio (peer-to-peer matching share), and solver activity.

Module: execution

Status: approved

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/execution/cow/marts/fct_execution_cow_daily.sql

Tags: production execution cow daily microbatch

Columns

NameTypeDescription
active_solversNullable(UInt64)Number of distinct solvers that settled batches on this day.
cow_ratioFloat64Share of batches that were pure CoW (peer-to-peer).
dateDateCalendar day of the aggregated CoW Protocol metrics.
fees_usdNullable(Float64)Total surplus-based protocol fees in USD on this day (fee_source = 'api', Sep 2024+).
num_batchesNullable(UInt64)Number of settlement batches on this day.
num_cow_batchesNullable(UInt64)Batches settled via pure Coincidence of Wants (no AMM interactions).
num_tradesUInt64Number of trade fills settled on this day.
solver_value_usdNullable(Float64)Daily gross value found by solvers (priceImprovement + surplus policies, Sep 2024+).
total_interactionsNullable(UInt64)Total number of external contract calls (AMM swaps) across all batches on this day.
total_tx_cost_nativeNullable(Float64)Total gas cost in xDAI across all batches.
unique_tradersUInt64Distinct taker (order owner) addresses trading on this day.
volume_usdNullable(Float64)Total USD trade volume on this day.
