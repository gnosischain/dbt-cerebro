dim_time_spine_weekly

ISO-week (Monday-start) time spine, used as the cross-sector join bridge for weekly metrics (e.g. revenue, gpay, p2p weekly aggregates). One row per Monday between Gnosis Chain genesis and five years past today. Built as a deduped projection of dim_time_spine_daily so granularity boundaries always agree.
