int_ubo_claims_sdai_daily

Per-protocol UBO supply claims for the sDAI ERC4626 vault (0xaf204776c7245bf4147c2612bf6e5972ee483701) on Gnosis Chain. Each sDAI holder's proportional claim on the WxDAI held by the vault is computed as (sDAI_balance / total_sDAI_supply) × vault_WxDAI_reserve. Holder balances are sourced directly from int_execution_tokens_balances_daily (no Transfer event parsing needed). Affects the WxDAI top-holders view; the sDAI token top-holders view is unaffected. Note: aGnosDAI (0x7a5c38...) appears as a second-level container — its share is left attributed to the aToken address rather than individual Aave lenders.
