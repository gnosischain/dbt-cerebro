int_execution_circles_v2_economically_active_avatars_weekly

Circles-first ECONOMICALLY ACTIVE avatars, ecosystem-wide — one row per (week, avatar, earning_kind) where the avatar earned >= 1 unit of a Circles reward that week: gcrc_cashback (>= 1 gCRC from the cashback wallet) or inviter_fee (>= 1 CRC of invitation fees). Deliberately NOT filtered to any app: the Gnosis App WEAU derives from this layer downstream by filtering to in-app users/actions (int_execution_gnosis_app_weekly_earners).
