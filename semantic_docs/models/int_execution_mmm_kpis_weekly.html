int_execution_mmm_kpis_weekly

Long-form weekly KPI registry for the Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) pipeline. One row per (`week`, `kpi_name`) on a continuous weekly spine generated by the `weekly_spine` macro over the trailing 730 days. Every (week, kpi) pair within the registry's active range is materialised even when the underlying source has no data: weeks are filled with 0 for `kpi_value_method = 'sum'` and NULL for `kpi_value_method IN ('last', 'avg', 'weighted_avg')` so the downstream wide pivot in `fct_execution_mmm_spine_weekly` never sees a hole that would silently truncate a regression window. Source aggregators (one CTE each, weekly grain): pools_tvl_usd ← argMax(tvl_usd, date) over fct_execution_pools_daily (last-value snapshot — TVL is a stock, summing over time is meaningless) pools_volume_usd ← sum(volume_usd_daily) over fct_execution_pools_daily dex_volume_usd_dedup ← sum(volume_usd) over fct_execution_trades_by_protocol_daily. First-hop-only acknowledgement: per-tx dedup on int_execution_pools_dex_trades OOM'd at the 10 GiB cluster cap, so the known multi-hop overcount is accepted and flagged via `is_dedup_safe = false` in the registry (and surfaced on the row). ga_active_users ← argMax(active_users, date) over fct_execution_gnosis_app_users_daily ga_new_users ← sum(new_users) gpay_topups_count ← sum(n_topups) over fct_execution_gnosis_app_gpay_topups_weekly gpay_topups_volume_usd ← sum(volume_usd) Adding a new KPI: append to seed `mmm_kpi_registry.csv` (drives the spine fill) AND add an aggregator CTE here AND add a `sumIf` clause in the wide pivot at `fct_execution_mmm_spine_weekly`. Incremental on `week` (monthly partition).
