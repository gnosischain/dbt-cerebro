int_execution_circles_v2_mint_events

Per-event view over int_execution_circles_v2_hub_transfers restricted to mint events (from_address = 0x00…00), tagged with a `mint_kind` to distinguish personal mints, group mints, and V1→V2 migrations. All three look identical at the ERC-1155 layer; classification combines the structural shape of the transfer (`token_address = to_address`) with the avatar registry (`avatar_type`) and a join against top-level V2 Hub `migrate()` calls. Classifier: personal - token_avatar_type = 'Human' AND token_address = to_address. Human avatar mints their own Circles to themselves — either via a direct personalMint() call or as the unscheduled mint that the Hub performs during a transfer. group - token_avatar_type = 'Group'. Group CRC minted; covers both group-to-depositor and group-to-self shapes. migration - tx contains a top-level V2 Hub migrate() call. Historically 4 calls, 0 emitted mints; kept in the enum so the classifier remains structurally correct. other - residual; should be ~0. Presence indicates a missing avatar registration row. Incremental, monthly partitioned on block_timestamp.
