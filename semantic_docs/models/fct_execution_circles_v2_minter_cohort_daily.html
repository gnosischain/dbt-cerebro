fct_execution_circles_v2_minter_cohort_daily

Daily cohort distribution of Circles v2 minters bucketed by 14-day mint coverage (mint_14dw / 336). Six buckets: <1%, [1-20%[, [20-40%[, [40-60%[, [60-80%[, +80%. Blacklisted avatars are excluded via LEFT JOIN to stg_crawlers_data__circles_blacklisted, matching the Dune circles-v2-kpis chart.
