stg_pools__balancer_v3_events

Unified Balancer V3 Vault event stream normalized into a single row-shape: (pool_address | wrapped_token_address, block_timestamp, transaction_hash, log_index, event_type, token_index | token_address, delta_amount_raw, fee_amount_raw). Source: contracts_BalancerV3_Vault_events. LiquidityAdded/Removed are exploded by ARRAY JOIN over the amountsAdded/Removed arrays (one row per pool token). Swap emits two rows (positive tokenIn, negative tokenOut). Wrap/Unwrap/LiquidityAddedToBuffer events are emitted with wrapped_token_address populated and pool_address NULL. Delta sign convention: positive = tokens entering the pool/wrapper, negative = leaving.
