fct_mixpanel_ga_gpay_acquisition_weekly

Weekly progression of Gnosis Pay first-funded accounts and first card transactions, broken down by Mixpanel UTM campaign. Tidy/long shape: one row per (week, event_type, attribution_model, utm_campaign). event_type is 'funded' or 'first_payment'; attribution_model is 'first_touch' or 'last_touch'. Attribution logic: an account's UTM comes from joining its on-chain pseudonym to Mixpanel (user_pseudonym == user_id_hash) and reading the per-user first/last-touch campaign from int_mixpanel_ga_user_acquisition. First-touch = campaign on the account's earliest utm-tagged event, last-touch = latest, derived by ordering each user's events on event_time (UTM is sparse — only ~3% of events carry it, on entry/landing hits). Accounts with no Mixpanel UTM fall to 'unknown' so totals reconcile to the full funnel. Aggregate-only (campaign-level counts, no user identifiers). Not exposed via cerebro-api (inherited exclusion); accessible to the MCP / semantic layer via the api_ view.
