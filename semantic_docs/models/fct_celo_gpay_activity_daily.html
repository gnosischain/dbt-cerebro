fct_celo_gpay_activity_daily

Daily card-payment activity (active Safes, payment count, USD volume) plus newly-funded and cumulative-funded card counts. "Funded" == a Safe that has settled at least one Payment to the bridge (== int_celo_gpay_wallets.is_activated). User grain is safe_address (on Celo a card = a Safe = the user). Current partial day is excluded. USDC/USDT only.
