int_execution_gnosis_app_gpay_topups

Gnosis App → Gnosis Pay top-ups. A top-up = a Gnosis Pay "Crypto Deposit" into a GP wallet that is currently GA-owned, per the persistent int_execution_gnosis_app_gpay_wallets bridge (is_currently_ga_owned), from the GA launch (2025-11-12) on. ga_user is the wallet's first GA owner. This supersedes the original same-transaction CoW-trade + deposit rule, which captured only ~1.4% of GA-owned funding — most top-ups are direct EURe transfers, not atomic swaps. See docs/model_review/gpay_topup_capture_probe.md. Scope (broadest): all Crypto Deposits into currently-GA-owned wallets, all tokens. Two narrowing toggles are intentionally NOT applied and can be added as a one-line WHERE: self-funding exclusion (counterparty = the wallet's GA owner) and a temporal filter for deposits predating GA ownership of an imported wallet.
