fct_mixpanel_ga_campaign_composition

Campaign COMPOSITION (state-based, not weekly) — the current make-up of each first-touch campaign's users from the Mixpanel profile snapshot: how many are PWA installs, have an IBAN, hold TheG NFT, are Circles backers, joined via referral, and how many went on to fund a Gnosis Pay card (via the profile `pay` bridge). Complements fct_mixpanel_ga_campaign_funnel_weekly (timed conversions) with cohort make-up. Profile flags are current-state, so these are "is currently" rates, not timed. K-anonymity: campaigns with < 5 users bucketed into '_small_campaigns'. Aggregate-only output.
