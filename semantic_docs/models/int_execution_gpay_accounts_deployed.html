int_execution_gpay_accounts_deployed

True Gnosis Pay account universe, one row per deployed account. Unlike int_execution_gpay_wallets (payment-gated), accounts are identified by the deploy-time on-chain signature: a Safe that enabled a Gnosis Pay Zodiac module (Delay / Roles / Spender mastercopy), independent of whether it ever made a card payment - so this also counts deployed-but-never-spent accounts. June 2026 migrated Safes are collapsed onto their canonical NEW Safe (via int_execution_gpay_safe_canonical) and keep the earliest deploy time.
