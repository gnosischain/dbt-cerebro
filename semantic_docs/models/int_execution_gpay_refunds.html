int_execution_gpay_refunds

Exploit-recovery refunds credited to migrated NEW Safes by the distributor wallets in the gpay_refund_distributors seed. Refunds arrive days after migration completion, not at Safe deployment. refund_date is the per-(new Safe, token) cutover used by int_execution_gpay_balances_user_daily: from that date the paired OLD Safe's residual balance for the token is recovery-entitled and excluded from user-holdings aggregates.
