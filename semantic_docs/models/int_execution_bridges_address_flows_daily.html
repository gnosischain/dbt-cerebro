int_execution_bridges_address_flows_daily

Address-grain bridge flows. Joins whitelisted transfers against the dune labels set filtered on bridge projects to extract one row per (date, bridge_contract, user_address, token, direction). Enables the Graph Explorer to render (address -> bridge) edges that the aggregate chain-level bridge models do not expose.

Module: execution

Status: candidate

Owner: bridges_team

Path: models/execution/transfers/intermediate/int_execution_bridges_address_flows_daily.sql

Tags: execution transfers bridges graph_explorer

Columns

NameTypeDescription
amount_raw_sumFloat64Sum of raw token amounts for the (user, bridge, token, direction, date) bucket.
bridge_contractString0x-prefixed bridge contract address (either the from or to leg of the transfer).
bridge_nameStringProject label for the bridge (e.g. 'Hop', 'Across', 'xDai Bridge') from dune labels.
dateDateDay bucket of the transfer leg.
directionString'in' when the bridge is on the from side (user receives), 'out' when on the to side (user sends to bridge).
symbolStringToken symbol.
token_addressStringERC20 token address being bridged.
transfer_countUInt64Number of transfers aggregated into this bucket.
user_addressStringThe non-bridge counterparty of the transfer (the user).
volume_usdNullable(Float64)USD volume (currently NULL — placeholder for when whitelisted_daily carries USD).
