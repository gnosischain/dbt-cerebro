fct_execution_pools_daily

Daily pool-level metrics for top Uniswap V3 and Swapr V3 pools on Gnosis Chain, including TVL (USD), accrued fees, and 7D trailing fee APR. Filtered to top 5 pools per token by 30-day average TVL.
