int_revenue_sdai_fees_daily

Daily per-user sDAI revenue accruing to the DAO. Methodology: `fees = balance_usd * sdai_daily_rate * dao_share_pct`, where `sdai_daily_rate` comes from int_yields_sdai_rate_daily and `dao_share_pct` (10%) is the share of the full rate assumed to accrue to the DAO. Tracks native sDAI balances (int_execution_tokens_balances_daily) plus sDAI held in Aave V3 (aGnosDAI) from int_execution_lending_aave_user_balances_daily — where `symbol = 'sDAI'` represents the aGnosDAI aToken holder. SparkLend is excluded.
