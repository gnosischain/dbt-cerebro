contracts_ocsdai_events

Decoded event logs for the OpenCover OC-sDAI vault ("Covered Savings xDAI", CoveredMetavault, 0x0ac34fe133bde3a2ef589a18a4e10b6a7d253829). ERC-4626 / async ERC-7540 vault whose asset() is sDAI. Standard ERC-4626 Deposit(sender,owner,assets,shares) and Withdraw(...,assets,shares) events are decoded here and consumed by int_yields_ocsdai_share_price_daily to reconstruct the vault share price for the revenue sDAI look-through.
