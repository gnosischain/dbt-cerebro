api_consensus_entry_queue_daily

The api_consensus_entry_queue_daily model provides daily aggregated statistics on the validator entry queue, enabling analysis of queue size and distribution over time for consensus monitoring.

Module: consensus

Status: candidate

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/consensus/marts/api_consensus_entry_queue_daily.sql

Tags: production consensus tier1 api:entry_queue granularity:daily

Columns

NameTypeDescription
dateDateThe specific calendar date for the recorded metrics.
meanFloat64Average queue size across all validators for the given date.
q05Float645th percentile of the entry queue size, indicating the lower bound of queue length distribution.
q10Float6410th percentile of the entry queue size, representing the lower tail of the distribution.
q25Float6425th percentile (first quartile) of the queue size distribution.
q50Float64Median queue size, representing the middle value of the distribution.
q75Float6475th percentile (third quartile) of the queue size, indicating the upper quartile.
q90Float6490th percentile of the queue size, showing the upper tail of the distribution.
q95Float6495th percentile of the queue size, highlighting the extreme upper values.
validator_countUInt64Number of validators active on the given date.
