fct_yields_savings_xdai_apy_daily

Canonical Savings xDAI APY mart. Long-format output with one row per (date, label) where label ∈ {'Daily','7DMA','30DMA','7DMM','30DMM'}. APY is compounded from the proper daily_rate in int_yields_savings_xdai_rate_daily (a true ratio, not a diff). 7-day and 30-day moving averages and moving medians are computed via window aggregates on the compounded annual rate. Regime columns (canonical_label, legacy_symbol, backing_asset, yield_source) propagate so consumers can distinguish pre/post-2025-11-07 rows (DAI/sDAI vs USDS/sUSDS backing) without joining again.
