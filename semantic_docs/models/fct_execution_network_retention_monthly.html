fct_execution_network_retention_monthly

Per-cohort monthly network retention. A cohort is the set of addresses whose first successful transaction landed in a given month; retention is the share of that cohort with at least one further active day inside the 30/90/180-day window after first_seen_date. Fills the new-vs-returning-wallet ambiguity that bare DAU/transaction-count charts cannot resolve. The 180d column for the most recent cohorts continues to refine as new daily activity lands.
