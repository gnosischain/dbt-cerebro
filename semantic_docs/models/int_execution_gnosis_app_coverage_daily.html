int_execution_gnosis_app_coverage_daily

Per-day, per-conversion-kind tracked-coverage stats. The MTA persona reads this to print the coverage block ("X% of topups on date D had ≥1 touchpoint in the prior 30 days") without re-running the conversion × events self-join. "Tracked" definition: a conversion has at least one row in `int_execution_gnosis_app_user_events_unified` whose `event_ts` falls in `[conversion_ts - 30d, conversion_ts)` AND whose `event_kind` is not the conversion's own mapped event_kind (leakage guard via the `conversion_kind_to_event_kind` macro — a `topup` conversion can't be tracked by its own `chain.topup` event). Incremental, partitioned by month on `conversion_date`. Uses `grace_hash` join algorithm in pre-hook because the events scan can span the full 30-day lookback per conversion-month batch.
