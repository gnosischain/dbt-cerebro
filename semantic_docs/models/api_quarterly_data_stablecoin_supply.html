api_quarterly_data_stablecoin_supply

Quarterly stablecoin supply statistics on Gnosis Chain, split by peg class (USD-pegged vs non-USD). Shows median, max, and average of daily supply across all days in the quarter. Excludes BRZ.

Module: quarterly_data

Status: candidate

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/quarterly_data/stablecoins/marts/api_quarterly_data_stablecoin_supply.sql

Tags: production quarterly_data tier0 api:stablecoin_supply granularity:quarterly

Columns

NameTypeDescription
peg_classStringStablecoin peg classification: 'USD-pegged' or 'non-USD'.
quarterDateQuarter start date.
supply_avgFloat64Average daily total supply (USD) across all days in the quarter.
supply_maxFloat64Maximum daily total supply (USD) observed in the quarter.
supply_medianFloat64Median daily total supply (USD) across all days in the quarter.
supply_minFloat64Minimum daily total supply (USD) observed in the quarter.
tokens_includedStringComma-separated list of tokens in this peg class.

Dimensions

  • quarter (time)
  • peg_class (categorical)
  • tokens_included (categorical)

Measures

  • supply_avg_value (average)
