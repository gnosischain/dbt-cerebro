api_quarterly_data_gpay_active_users

Peak monthly active users for Gnosis Pay within the quarter, on two bases: all-activity (peak_monthly_active_users) and payment-only (peak_monthly_payment_users). The payment basis is the one the quarterly report uses; the all-activity basis is inflated in June 2026 by the Safe-migration deposit/withdrawal churn.
