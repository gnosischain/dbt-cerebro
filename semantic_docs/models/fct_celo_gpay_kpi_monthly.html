fct_celo_gpay_kpi_monthly

Monthly KPIs - MAU (and per-action MAU), payment/deposit/withdrawal USD volumes, net flow (deposits minus withdrawals), ARPU, avg tx/user, repeat purchase rate. Deposit == Top-up, Withdrawal == Withdrawal. No cashback columns. Partial current month excluded. USDC/USDT only.

Module: celo

Status: docs_only

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/celo/gpay/marts/fct_celo_gpay_kpi_monthly.sql

Tags: production celo gpay
