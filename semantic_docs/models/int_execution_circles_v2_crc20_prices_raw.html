int_execution_circles_v2_crc20_prices_raw

Trade-level executed prices for CRC20 wrapper tokens across all supported DEX protocols. Filters int_execution_pools_dex_trades for swaps where either leg is a known CRC20 wrapper (joined from int_execution_circles_v2_wrapper_tokens). Balancer V2 trades are captured automatically via the single vault contract and need no whitelist entry. Uniswap V3 CRC20 pools require their addresses in contracts_whitelist.csv (type UniswapV3Pool). USD price is passed through directly from int_execution_pools_dex_trades.amount_usd, which already resolves the backing token price — no extra join is needed here. Dust trades with either leg below 1e-4 tokens are excluded to prevent tiny swaps from distorting prices.
