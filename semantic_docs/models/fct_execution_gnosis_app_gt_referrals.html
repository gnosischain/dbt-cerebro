fct_execution_gnosis_app_gt_referrals

Two DISTINCT referral metrics side by side: `earned` (GA-native paid-reward ledger) and `full_invite_graph` (every accepted invite, a ~2.2x superset with a heuristic twin int_execution_circles_v2_inviter_canonical). reward_usd is permanently NULL (CRC has no price feed). Never reconcile `earned` against circles_v2 inviter_fee.
