fct_execution_lending_top_lenders_ranked

Latest-day snapshot of lending suppliers ranked by USD balance within each (protocol, symbol) pair. Top 500 per pair. No joins — only window functions on int_execution_lending_aave_user_balances_daily. Materialized as a table to decouple the heavy scan from the lightweight joins in fct_execution_lending_top_lenders_latest.
