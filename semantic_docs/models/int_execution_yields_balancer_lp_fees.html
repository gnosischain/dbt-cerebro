int_execution_yields_balancer_lp_fees

True Balancer (V2/V3) LP swap fees per (provider, pool), used by int_execution_yields_user_lp_positions to replace the capital_out - capital_in PnL proxy (model review EXECUTIONYIELDS-C12). Monthly contribution-based, value-weighted attribution of int_execution_pools_fees_daily pool fees: each month's pool fees are split across LPs by cumulative net contributed USD (mint - burn) as of month-end. Conserves to pool totals.
