fct_bridges_netflow_weekly_by_bridge

This model aggregates weekly net flow data for each bridge, providing a time-series view of net USD transactions to support trend analysis and reporting.

Module: bridges

Status: candidate

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/bridges/marts/fct_bridges_netflow_weekly_by_bridge.sql

Tags: production intermediate bridges

Columns

NameTypeDescription
bridgeStringIdentifier for the bridge for which the net flow data is recorded.
cum_netflow_usdFloat64The cumulative net flow of USD for the bridge from the earliest available data up to the specified week.
netflow_usd_weekUInt64Total net USD flow for the bridge during the week, with missing values filled as zero.
weekDateTime64The starting date of the week (Monday) representing the aggregation period.

Dimensions

  • week (time)
  • bridge (categorical)

Measures

  • netflow_usd_week_value (sum)
  • cum_netflow_usd_value (sum)
