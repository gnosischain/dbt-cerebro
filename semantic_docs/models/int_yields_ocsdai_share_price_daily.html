int_yields_ocsdai_share_price_daily

Daily share price (sDAI per OC-sDAI share) for the OpenCover OC-sDAI vault ("Covered Savings xDAI", 0x0ac34fe133bde3a2ef589a18a4e10b6a7d253829), an ERC-4626 covered vault whose asset() is sDAI. Reconstructs end-of-day share price = assets / shares from the vault's ERC-4626 Deposit/Withdraw events (contracts_ocsdai_events), forward-filled across event-less days from the 2026-03-16 launch. Used by int_revenue_ocsdai_user_balances_daily to value OC-sDAI holders' underlying sDAI for the revenue sDAI stream. Emits only the share-price level (no APY/rate windows), unlike int_yields_savings_xdai_rate_daily.
