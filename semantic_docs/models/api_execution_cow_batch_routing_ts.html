api_execution_cow_batch_routing_ts

Daily share of CoW Protocol settlement batches by routing type, as percentages of total daily batches. Three labels: "Pure CoW" (multi-trade batch with zero AMM interactions — true Coincidence of Wants), "Partial CoW" (multi-trade batch with some AMM interactions, meaning at least some peer matching happened), "Pure DEX" (single-trade batches or all trades routed through AMMs). Pure CoW peaked at ~34% in Sep 2021 and is near zero on Gnosis Chain since mid-2022.
