contracts_GBCDeposit_events

The contracts_GBCDeposit_events model captures and processes deposit event logs related to the GBC Deposit contract, providing a detailed record of deposit activities over time for analytics and reporting purposes.

Module: contracts

Status: docs_only

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/contracts/GBCDeposit/contracts_GBCDeposit_events.sql

Tags: production contracts gbcdeposit events

Columns

NameTypeDescription
block_numberUInt64Blockchain block number where the event was recorded.
block_timestampDateTime64(0, 'UTC')Timestamp of the block containing the event, in UTC.
contract_addressStringAddress of the contract emitting the event.
deposit_amountStringAmount deposited, in wei.
deposit_timestampDateTime64(0, 'UTC')Timestamp when the deposit was made, in UTC.
deposit_tokenStringToken type of the deposit.
depositor_addressStringAddress of the account making the deposit.
event_typeStringType of event, e.g., 'Deposit'.
log_indexUInt64Index of the log within the block, used to order events.
statusStringStatus of the deposit event, e.g., 'success' or 'failed'.
transaction_feeStringFee paid for the transaction, in wei.
transaction_hashStringUnique identifier of the transaction in which the event occurred.
