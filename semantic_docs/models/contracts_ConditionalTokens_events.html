contracts_ConditionalTokens_events

The `contracts_ConditionalTokens_events` model captures and stores event logs related to Conditional Tokens contracts, enabling analysis of contract interactions and event occurrences over time.

Module: contracts

Status: docs_only

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/contracts/ConditionalTokens/contracts_ConditionalTokens_events.sql

Tags: production contracts conditionaltokens events

NameTypeDescription

condition_typeStringType of condition (e.g., binary, scalar).

created_atDateTime64(0, 'UTC')Timestamp when the record was created in the database, in UTC.

outcome_indexUInt64Index of the outcome related to the event.
outcome_valueStringValue or description of the outcome.
participant_addressStringAddress of the participant involved in the event.
transaction_hashStringHash of the transaction that emitted the event.
transaction_indexUInt64Index of the transaction within the block.
