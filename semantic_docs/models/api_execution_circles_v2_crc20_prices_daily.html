api_execution_circles_v2_crc20_prices_daily

Public daily price view for CRC20 wrapper tokens, consolidated across all pools. One row per (date, crc20_token) with a volume-weighted average USD price (VWAP). Only tokens with at least one DEX trade on a given day appear; tokens without market liquidity have no rows.

Module: execution

Status: approved

Owner: analytics_team

Path: models/execution/Circles/marts/api_execution_circles_v2_crc20_prices_daily.sql

Columns

price_median_usdNullable(Float64)Median of per-pool daily median prices in USD.
price_vwap_usdNullable(Float64)Volume-weighted average price in USD across all pools on this day.
symbolNullable(String)Display symbol for the CRC20 token.

total_crc_sold_volumeFloat64Total CRC20 tokens sold (CRC was the token_sold leg) across all pools on this day.
total_crc_volumeFloat64Total CRC20 tokens traded across all pools on this day.
