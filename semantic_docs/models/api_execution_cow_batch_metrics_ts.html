api_execution_cow_batch_metrics_ts

Daily share of CoW Protocol settlement batches by routing type. Three labels: "Pure CoW" (multi-trade batch with zero AMM interactions — true Coincidence of Wants), "Partial CoW" (multi-trade batch where num_trades > num_interactions, indicating at least some peer matching happened), "Pure DEX" (single-trade batches plus multi-trade batches fully routed through AMMs). Pure CoW peaked at ~34% in Oct 2021 and is near zero on Gnosis Chain since 2022.
