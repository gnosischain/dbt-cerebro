fct_execution_circles_v2_inviter_farm_quota

Per-inviter Circles-v2 InvitationFarm ("invitation-at-scale") state: invites claimed from the farm and latest granted quota. Complements int_execution_circles_v2_inviter_canonical (who invited whom, via InvitationModule.originInviter) with the farm's inviter activity/allowance. One row per inviter seen in InvitesClaimed or InviterQuotaUpdated.
