gpay_distinct_users

Distinct user_pseudonym count from int_execution_gpay_safe_identities — i.e. unique humans who own / delegate / control at least one Gnosis Pay Safe. Join with revenue per-user metrics on `user_pseudonym` to compute overlaps (e.g. "revenue-active users who are also gpay users"). Cardinality is per-pseudonym, not per-Safe; one user can manage many Safes (initial_owner / delegate / safe_self roles).
